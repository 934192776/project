I love dance

Especially Ballet

Ballet is a light, lively and romantic dance. Ballet originated in Italy but flourished in France. Some of its dances can be traced back to the dances of ancient Greece. Ballet is the transliteration of the French "ballet", and its etymology is the Italian "balletto", which is the diminutive word for ballo, which means "dance". Ballet is a kind of modern musical accompaniment with unpretentious costumes and stage scenery.
